My Life

My Professional Life

Hi there! My name is Anthony and I'll tell you a little bit about my professional life. I graduated from Tufts University in May of 2016 with a degree in Civil Engineering and a minor in Engineering Management. I'm currently making my rotations through Liberty Mutual's Technical Developement Program. In my first 6-month rotation, I worked as a User Experience (UX) researcher for the Digital Experience Group in Boston, Massachusetts. We focused on using lean method and Design thinking approaches to understand the user mindset. In my current rotation in Portsmouth, New Hampshire, I'm a Data and Services lead on a five person Agile team. We're focused on creating a new UI and migrating 1 billion documents into an online document repostitory.
